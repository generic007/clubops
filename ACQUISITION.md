## Objection Handling

**"We already use QuickBooks":**
ClubOps isn't an accounting replacement — it's an operations layer for poker-specific needs. It integrates with existing accounting. The ledger tracks chips, rake, promotions, and comps that QuickBooks doesn't understand.

**"We use Club OS":**
Club OS is designed for fitness clubs, not poker. ClubOps has raked-hand tracking, player blacklists (self-exclusion), bad-beat jackpot calculation, and regulatory compliance reports built in.

**"Too expensive":**
$99/mo is less than 2 hours of a floor manager's time per week. If ClubOps saves 5 hours/week of manual work, you're saving $300+/mo in labor.

**"We just use spreadsheets":**
Spreadsheets don't have immutable ledgers. If there's a dispute with a player about their balance, ClubOps provides an audit-proof trail. Spreadsheets can be changed silently.
